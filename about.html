About us

The Bald Guy, the Curly Guy, and a Whole Lot of Arizona Windows.

We're a family-owned window and glass company built on a frustratingly simple idea: do the work right, charge a fair price, answer your phone, and stand behind every install. Forever.

Our story

Built in Arizona. For Arizona.

We started Bald & Curly Glass because we got tired of watching homeowners get steamrolled by national chains pitching the same vinyl frame from a script — windows engineered for Cleveland, sold in Phoenix.

Arizona's a different climate. 115° summers, monsoon dust, intense UV — your windows take a beating most of the country never sees. So we built a company that specs every window like the desert is actually trying to get inside (because it is).

We're local. We pick up the phone. The guy who quotes your job is the guy on the install. And if anything we put in ever leaks, sticks, or fogs, the same number that quoted you fixes it.
